Hello, wanderer,
I am Mr. Rock,
Here lies a photograph of me.

For it highlights my face,
But it is faded,
The voice is lost.

Now, fair exchange,
A glimpse for a glimpse,
What will you show me?

Are you done yet?
I waited a while.
You'll be regretful soon.
 Ah, observer.
I perceive you,
Highlighting this place.
